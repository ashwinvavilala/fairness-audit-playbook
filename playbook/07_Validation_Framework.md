\# Validation Framework



\## Purpose

Ensure fairness audits are effective, repeatable, and trustworthy.



\## Validation Dimensions



\### A. Process Validation

\- Was each component completed?  

\- Were decisions documented?  

\- Were intersectional groups included?  



\### B. Technical Validation

\- Are metrics statistically reliable?  

\- Are subgroup sizes sufficient?  

\- Are results reproducible?  



\### C. Outcome Validation

\- Did disparities decrease?  

\- Did performance remain acceptable?  

\- Were mitigations effective?  



\### D. Governance Validation

\- Were results reviewed by stakeholders?  

\- Are audit artifacts stored centrally?  

\- Is there a re-audit schedule?  



\## Output

A validation summary included in the final audit report.
